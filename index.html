UNIFORMS, MONOTONOUS OR UNANIMOUS?

24 August 2021

Positive and negative aspects of school uniforms: a safety pattern or a restriction on expression.

Recently in my school, there are some protests about the mandatory use of school uniforms. We have all ever complained about the typical boring colors and how uncomfortable uniforms can be, but these protests go further: "Freedom of expression in our way of dressing! We want to wear our own colors!" Students believe that it is necessary to create a change that allows them to express their personality through their appearance, while school officials believe that the use of uniforms is safer and more disciplinary. For this situation, I share some points for and against the mandatory uniforms in schools that could help you decide a side in the protest.

Negative Aspects

The following factors are those that are limited thanks to the mandatory uniforms according to the school community:

Personal expression

The main reason why uniforms are criticized is for representing a limitation on freedom of expression. The fact that all students wear the same clothes prevents the possibility of differentiating themselves from the rest; at the same time, uniforms create a monotonous and boring environment that students are required to be a part of.

Accessibility

Uniforms can be expensive and uncomfortable. The accessibility of uniforms has always been limited to the standard; however, those people with physical disabilities who require comfortable or special clothing face a great problem when it comes to wearing uniforms. On the other hand, uniforms are economic barriers that should not exist in education.

Cultural Identity

Clothing is part of the culture and must be respected. For religious, social and personal reasons, there are cases in which students need to wear different clothing than the uniform requires. For this reason, there must be flexibility regarding clothing, according to the cultural situation of each student.

Positive Aspects

Below you will find the arguments that my school has presented in favor of the mandatory uniform:

Security

Uniforms are useful for identifying students in a school; in this way, they prevent outsiders from entering and causing problem, also uniforms allow teachers to recognize students outside their classrooms. Additionally, uniforms help drivers to be aware of school zones.

Belonging

Uniforms allow us to distinguish ourselves as members of a community and create unanimity among the students, avoiding inequalities. In addition, this unanimity favors a feeling of belonging that generates respect and comfort for being part of a certain institution.

Discipline

Discipline is fostered through the responsibility that comes with complying with a dress code. Uniforms must be the best expression of a responsible and organized student body; in this case, uniforms reflect the rigor and cleanliness of a school and therefore it is convenient for schools to use them to demonstrate these values.

Opinion

Personally, I think that uniforms are not completely a negative factor, as they are more practical and safer than free clothing; also, wearing my school uniform makes me feel proud of where I study and the people who study with me. However, it is true that in some situations, uniforms represent a limitation which depends on the strictness of the school regulations. I think that uniforms are preferable if they do not imply a limitation to the culture or needs of the students.

After analyzing the positive and negative factors of the mandatory use of school uniforms, do you consider that they are necessary? what would you do if you were the principal of the school? share your answers on the forum!
